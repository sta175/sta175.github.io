# DataFest Schedule: Overview 

DataFest begins on Friday April 4 at 5:30 PM and continues until Sunday April 6 at 1 PM. 

- Friday: Getting Started
- Saturday: Work Day
- Sunday: Judging and Announcing the Winners

## Location: TBA
